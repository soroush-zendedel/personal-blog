# Tried My Best

How cruel you are—  
yet strangely so,  
no pride in that,  
I know, I know.  

But still it shows  
and makes it clear:  
I shouldn't hope  
while you're still here.  

And just to know—  
that truth alone  
is worth the pain  
I've always known.  

No matter how  
I brace my heart,  
your leaving tears me  
all apart.  

I gave you all  
I couldn't name,  
a love too quiet  
to call or claim.  

You turned away,  
and still I stay—  
half of me  
won't fade away.  

No hope remains  
that you might see  
the quiet love  
that lives in me.  

My heart still plays  
your cruel game;  
Love has changed,  
and lost its name.  

One day the loneliest  
of all,  
one day the one  
who dared to fall.  

But tell me how  
to walk away,  
when every road  
still leads your way.  

I learned that love  
and loss entwine,  
that every wound  
was once divine.  

So keep your peace,  
and I'll keep mine—  
two hearts apart,  
one broken line.  

So if I break,  
I'll break alone,  
I'll carry what  
I've always known.  

This call repeats  
in my chest:  
I tried my best…  
I tried my best.
