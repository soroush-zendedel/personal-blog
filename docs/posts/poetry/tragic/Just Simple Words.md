# Just Simple Words

A single step,  
I pass you by,  
A silent storm  
inside your eye.  

This is the way  
the story ends,  
No longer lovers,  
barely friends.  

My heart aches  
just to turn around,  
A heavy word  
without a sound.  

This silence is  
a heavy stone,  
A wall we built  
all on our own.  

I see the coldness  
in your gaze,  
A frozen, dark,  
and empty maze.  

I can't believe  
you feel so numb,  
Look at the strangers  
we've become.  

This isn't fate,  
it's only pride,  
That built the wall  
we hide behind.  

We had the choice,  
we had the voice,  
But silence was  
our only choice.  

So just pretend,  
just tell a lie,  
Just say,  
"I never loved you," try!  

A simple word  
before I go?  
The silence simply  
answers, "No."
