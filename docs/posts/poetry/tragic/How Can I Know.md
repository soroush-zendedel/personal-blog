# How Can I Know

Your voice still echoes  
in my mind,  
though your eyes  
are far from me.  

Why do you try  
to prove each time  
the moon must shine  
in vanity?  

You seem too cold,  
but don't you know  
I miss the fire  
that makes me glow?  

You turned me into  
someone whole,  
a heart that breaks,  
yet won't let go.  

There's someone different  
in my mind,  
from the one  
I think is you.  

I can't recall  
which one designed  
the plan to leave—  
how can I know?  

Where did you go?  
The sunlight hides,  
Two restless hearts  
on different sides.  

It's not its fault—  
the sun's like me,  
It burns for what  
it cannot see.  

I never know  
just what you need,  
or what you think  
I'm lacking now.  

Since you laughed  
that day at me,  
a quiet grief grew—  
don't ask how.  

A distance spreads  
between the spark  
of laughter and  
the tears that fall.  

Where is that  
prideful tear I knew—  
the one that faced  
the world through all?  

I want you just  
to be like me—  
a lover lost,  
a wild one too,  

who keeps a promise  
faithfully  
and stays until  
the world is through.
