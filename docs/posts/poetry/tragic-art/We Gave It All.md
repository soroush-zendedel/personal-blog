# We Gave It All

Lately you've been  
quiet, cold,  
Storms behind  
the eyes you hold.  

Memories tell us  
to stay:  
"Let it be,  
don't walk away."  

If you stay  
or if you leave,  
Nothing promised,  
we believed.  

Words like "always"  
weren't true;  
Promises can  
fail us too.  

Do you want  
the dreams erased?  
If you go,  
I won't resist.  

Every road  
you choose to run  
Carries me  
in every one.  

I won't stand  
before your way;  
Go to where  
your thoughts will stay.  

You're the one  
who taught me then  
How to lose  
and call it "when."  

Now our words  
destroy and hide,  
Only ghosts  
of love reside.  

In the shape  
we're living through,  
Separation  
feels like truth.  

Now our words  
destroy and hide,  
Only ghosts  
of love reside.  

In the shape  
we're living through,  
Separation  
feels like truth.  

I won't cage  
the air you need;  
Distance helps  
your heart to beat.  

Love can fade  
and love can fall;  
Everything returns  
to fall.  

Lately every word  
is sharp,  
Cuts the light  
and breaks the heart.  

If you broke  
my chest to see,  
There'd be nothing  
left of me.  

Reason gone,  
and madness near,  
Flowing strong  
instead of fear.  

In the silence  
of our breath,  
We remain  
in what is left.  

Paper waiting,  
pen in place,  
Just a name  
to end this space.  

Sign it now,  
don't tempt the wait;  
Maybe leaving's  
part of fate.  

Now our words  
destroy and hide,  
Only ghosts  
of love reside.  

In the shape  
we're living through,  
Separation  
feels like truth.  

Now our words  
destroy and hide,  
Only ghosts  
of love reside.  

In the shape  
we're living through,  
Separation  
feels like truth.  

Or maybe  
this isn't real,  
Just a dream  
I cannot feel.  

Maybe I'm still  
lying there,  
Body quiet…  
mind aware.
