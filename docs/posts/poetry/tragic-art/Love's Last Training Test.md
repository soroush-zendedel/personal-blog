# Love's Last Training Test

How am I  
to hold you back,  
When the shadows  
paint you black?  

Just a moment  
without lines—  
Can we let it  
all unwind?  

I would sit  
and stay right here  
Till your breathing  
disappears.  

Every quiet  
minute near  
Told me:  
"Die before the tear."  

It was never  
ours to keep,  
Fate had plans  
beneath the deep.  

Safe your journey,  
take your rest,  
This was love's  
last training test.  

Heavy madness,  
choking tight,  
Whispers softly  
in the night:  

"No forgiveness  
left to learn,  
Let this lesson  
wash and burn."  

This is choice,  
not destiny,  
You can go—  
your way is free.  

You're still near,  
but I can see—  
I'm already  
losing me.  

It was never  
ours to keep,  
Fate had plans  
beneath the deep.  

Safe your journey,  
take your rest,  
This was love's  
last training test.  

Hard to grasp  
or understand,  
You're the one  
letting my hand.  

Life without you  
holds no key—  
You're the root  
inside of me.  

Yet the damage  
that was done  
Pulled our fibers  
one by one.  

Go now, darling—  
let it be—  
Or I'll lose  
the rest of me.  

It was never  
ours to keep,  
Fate had plans  
beneath the deep.  

Safe your journey,  
take your rest,  
This was love's  
last training test.
