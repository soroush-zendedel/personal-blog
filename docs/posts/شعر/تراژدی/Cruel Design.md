# Cruel Design

I still recall  
that endless day,  
the one I fell  
and lost my way.  

The threads of fate  
were torn apart,  
and coldness settled  
in my heart.  

These thoughts repeat,  
they circle tight,  
what do you feel  
in dead of night?  

My silence screams  
your lost refrain;  
you looked, but never  
felt the same.  

I fell through dreams  
I couldn't keep,  
my wounds awake  
while others sleep.  

The fire inside  
refused to die;  
it burned beneath  
a silent sky.  

I still recall  
that endless day,  
the one I fell  
and lost my way.  

I still recall  
that endless day,  
I came to you,  
you turned away.  

My mind's a storm  
I can't contain,  
a rusted gear  
that ticks with pain.  

It played its tricks,  
its cruel design,  
but I stood firm  
and drew the line.  

One day my turn  
will come to rest,  
when war is done  
inside my chest.  

The mirror shows  
a stranger's face,  
a fading truth  
I can't erase.  

You're in the frames  
I can't erase,  
so take me to  
your fading place.  

If fate decreed  
this tragic art,  
then go—  
I'll guard my dying heart.  

I still recall  
that endless day,  
the one I fell  
and lost my way.  

I still recall  
that endless day,  
I came to you,  
you walked away.
