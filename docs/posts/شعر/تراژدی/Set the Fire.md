# Set the Fire

Set the fire —  
burn me clean,  
Feed the flame,  
I'll shape the scene.  

Don't kill the heat,  
don't fade away,  
Don't let shadows  
reign again.  

Set the fire —  
let it bloom,  
Ash to love,  
and love to gloom.  

From the scars  
beneath my skin,  
Let the light  
of me begin.  

Through the light,  
I see your face,  
Lost in fire's  
brief embrace.  

Chains of pain  
around me close,  
Burn to dust,  
like wilted rose.  

Make a lie  
I will believe,  
Love is right,  
it can deceive.  

You decide—  
just tell me where?  
Everywhere,  
I need you there.  

Set the fire —  
burn me clean,  
Feed the flame,  
I'll shape the scene.  

Don't kill the heat,  
don't fade away,  
Don't let darkness  
reign again.  

Echoed screams  
beneath my skin,  
Fade to songs  
of what has been.  

Let the pain  
stay and lie,  
Let me burn,  
but never die.  

Set the fire —  
one last time,  
Turn my loss  
to wordless rhyme.  

Cold remains,  
but fire stays,  
In the ash,  
the silence plays.
