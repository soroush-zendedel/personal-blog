# The Last Goodbye

The sky is dark  
and holds no light,  
And every star  
is lost to night.  

I search for what  
I cannot see,  
The hope that used  
to live in me.  

You're not the dream  
I used to chase,  
A nightmare came  
to take its place.  

Why must we prove  
what hearts can see?  
That pride makes strangers,  
you and me.  

You and me...  

So now we walk  
as strangers do,  
And gone is everything  
we knew.  

I can't believe  
that this is true,  
There is no "us",  
just me and you.  

I don't know what  
you need from me,  
I gave my all—  
what more could be?  

This quiet grief  
has left a void,  
And every hope  
I had destroyed.  

The promise broke  
along the way,  
A trace of sorrow  
came to stay.  

I reach to touch  
what used to be,  
But find a ghost  
in front of me.  

So now we walk  
as strangers do,  
And gone is everything  
we knew.  

I can't believe  
that this is true,  
There is no "us",  
just me and you.  

The time has come  
to turn the page,  
And close our eyes  
upon our rage.  

No other words  
are left to say,  
Just silence falls  
to mark the way.  

Now laughter fades  
and tears remain,  
The ghost of joy  
walks through the pain.  

Where is the strength  
to love, to try,  
To fall, yet still  
keep head held high?  

So now we walk  
as strangers do,  
And gone is everything  
we knew.  

I can't believe  
that this is true,  
There is no "us",  
just me and you.  

Just stay the same—  
be wild, be true,  
That endless love  
still reaches you.  

And if this vow  
must cross the skies,  
I'll keep it till  
the last goodbye.
