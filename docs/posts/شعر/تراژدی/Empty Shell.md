# Empty Shell

There was a time  
I understood  
Your every glance,  
your every mood.  

This captive heart  
you held so high  
Was where you let  
your pleasures fly.  

But you are gone,  
the joy is dead,  
A silent house  
is all I tread.  

This home of grief,  
this empty space,  
Once knew a smile  
upon your face.  

The world went dark  
the day you planned  
To leave this all  
for some new land.  

That heavy day  
you turned to go  
Was black with grief  
and deepest woe.  

This moving corpse,  
this empty shell,  
This hollow soul  
inside a hell,  

Was once a heart  
with fire and glow,  
But that was all  
so long ago.  

My mind is lost,  
my logic fades,  
I'm known by madness  
and its shades.  

There was a time,  
before the pain,  
A clear-eyed soul  
controlled my brain.  

You ask me of  
my past, my pride?  
The self I kept  
so deep inside?  

To just admit  
this love's control  
Feels like I'm selling  
off my soul.  

I see it now,  
that final gaze,  
You looked right through  
my painful daze.  

My heart is lost,  
you search no more,  
Though I was all  
you lived for, before.  

This moving corpse,  
this empty shell,  
This hollow soul  
inside a hell,  

Was once a heart  
with fire and glow,  
But that was all  
so long ago.
